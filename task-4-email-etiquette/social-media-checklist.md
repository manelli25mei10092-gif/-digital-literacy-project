### **Social Media Do’s:** 

\- Use strong and secure passwords 

\- Think before posting anything online 

\- Keep your profile professional 

\- Respect others and communicate politely 

\- Verify information before sharing 

\- Update your privacy settings regularly

&#x20;



### **Social Media Don’ts:**

&#x20;- Do not share personal or sensitive information

&#x20;- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content 

&#x20;- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links 

&#x20;- Do not spread fake news or misinformation

&#x20;- Avoid using weak passwords

&#x20;-Do not accept friend requests from unknown people
